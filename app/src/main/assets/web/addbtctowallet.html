Add Some BTC to Your Wallet

You can now add BTC to your wallet balance. Maybe a friend wants to pay you back for dinner. Or maybe you want to purchase BTC directly from an exchange, Whatever the case, you will want to share your wallet address with the sender. To do this, tap the Receive tab along the bottom of the screen.

Tap the Receive navigation tab to add BTC to your wallet.

Make sure the wallet displayed at the bottom of the screen is the one to which you or someone else should send BTC. To change it, simply tap that area and select the appropriate wallet.

To share this address with a sender, tap the share icon in the top right on the window, or tap the screen to copy the address to your clipboard. Once copied, you can paste it into an email or message app to share with the sender.

You can also simply show the QR code to the sender and have them “Scan” the image with their device. This is the most convenient way to give someone your Bitcoin address.
